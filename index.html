In Press

Obstetrical Factors Associated with Increased Mobilization of Hematopoietic Progenitor Cells in Umbilical Cord Blood

This research paper is in the process of publication. For this research project, a chart review was completed to study the factors associated with increased mobilization of hematopoietic stem and progenitor cells to allow strategic collection of the most potent units and improve cost efficiencies. After the data was collected, R software was used to perform univariable and multivariable analysis.
